Research

My research interests lie in Computer Vision, Generative AI, and Efficient LLMs. While my previous work focused on explainability in vision, my current research centers on efficient LLMs.

MSPCaps: A Multi-Scale Patchify Capsule Network with Cross-Agreement Routing for Visual Recognition
Yudong Hu, Yueju Han, Rui Sun, Jinke Ren,
AAAI, 2026
project page / arXiv

Our goal is to build a simpler, more explainable model for visual recognition. By extracting multi-scale features to initialize capsules, we enable effective part-to-whole voting mechanisms while keeping the architecture lightweight.
